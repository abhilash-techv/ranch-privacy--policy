Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Vadakkan Ranch collects, uses, and protects information from guests, website visitors, and booking enquiries.

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of May 5, 2026.

Information We Collect

Vadakkan Ranch may collect information guests provide directly, including name, phone number, email address, booking details, guest preferences, payment-related information (processed securely through third-party providers), and messages sent through our website, email, WhatsApp, or social media channels.

We may also collect technical information such as browser type, device type, website usage data, cookies, log data, and approximate location derived from IP address.

How We Use Information

  • To process reservations and booking enquiries.
  • To communicate check-in, check-out, and stay details.
  • To provide customer support and respond to questions.
  • To improve our services, guest experience, and website performance.
  • To send promotional offers and updates where permitted.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers, including payment processors, website hosting providers, analytics providers, and communication platforms used to manage bookings and guest services.

We may also disclose information when required by law, or where necessary to protect guests, our business, or prevent fraud, misuse, or security incidents.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill booking obligations, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. While no online platform can guarantee absolute security, we take appropriate measures to reduce risk.

Children's Privacy

Vadakkan Ranch services are intended for adults making accommodation bookings. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

Guests may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information by contacting us. Users may also opt out of non-essential communications.

International Users

If you access our website from outside India, your information may be processed and stored in India or in locations where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above. Continued use of our services means you accept the updated policy.
